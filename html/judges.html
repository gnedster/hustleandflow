    About:

    Open Judge CA is a project created as a part of Code For America’s Data Deathmatch Hackaton (11/2-11/3). In an effort to make public data more accessible, this project was powered by data from Fair Political Practice Commission's collection of the 2011 Statements of Economic Interests filed by many California Judges in 2012.

    Data disclaimer:

    This data is based on digitization by Capacity of the 2011 Statements of Economic Interests filings by most California Judges in 2012 and provided by the Fair Political Practices Commission in an effort to increase goverment transperency. This data may be subject to errors and gaps and should not be taken as a comprehensive data-set. For more information on the data, visit http://captricity.com/datadeathmatch or http://www.fppc.ca.gov
